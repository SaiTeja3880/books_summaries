You Can Win by Shiv Khera

The Book in Three Sentences

"You Can Win" is a self-help book by Shiv Khera. It is all about helping individuals to win in life by being positive and successful. The author explains how to become a winner in all aspects of life by changing your attitude and mindset. The author covers topics such as goal-setting, self-esteem, problem-solving, motivation, and communication. He provides practical tips and techniques for improving your life and achieving success. The author emphasizes that success is not just about achieving material wealth, but also about having a fulfilling and happy life. He encourages people to set high standards for themselves and to never give up on their dreams.
